About PSA

Samuel Amoo fondly called PSA is the pastor in charge of the redeemed Christian church of God, King’s Embassy parish Abuja. A graduate of mathematics from the Redeemers University with an MBA from the University of Southwales.

Born out of covenant with God his life’s calling to raise agents of light is expressed through the initiative of the Samuel Generation, an outreach that seeks to prove that pure breeds can arise in the midst of corruption
